# Customer Journey & Funnel Analysis Dashboard

## Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer behavior across the customer journey and identify opportunities to improve conversion rates, customer acquisition, customer retention, and overall business performance. The dashboard provides actionable insights for marketing, product, CRM, and management teams.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations invest significant resources in marketing campaigns to attract customers. However, not all visitors complete a purchase. Understanding where customers drop off during the buying journey is essential for improving conversions and maximizing revenue.

This project helps answer key business questions:

* Which acquisition channels generate the most customers?
* Where do customers drop off in the funnel?
* Which customer segments convert best?
* How do retention and feedback impact business performance?
* What actions can improve customer acquisition and retention?

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains customer journey and engagement data, including:

* Customer Information
* Customer Segment
* Acquisition Channel
* Marketing Campaign
* Website Activity
* Product Interaction
* Funnel Progression
* Purchase Details
* Session Metrics
* Customer Feedback
* Retention Status

The data simulates customer behavior from initial website visit through final purchase.

---

## Funnel Framework

The customer conversion funnel consists of five stages:

1. Website Visit
2. Product View
3. Add to Cart
4. Checkout Started
5. Purchase Completed

This framework helps identify conversion opportunities and customer drop-off points throughout the buying process.

## Key Insights

* Google Ads generated the highest customer traffic.
* Returning customers achieved higher conversion rates than new customers.
* The largest customer drop-off occurred between Add to Cart and Checkout stages.
* Customers with longer session durations demonstrated higher purchase likelihood.
* Retained customers contributed the majority of total revenue.

---

## Recommendations

* Optimize the checkout process to reduce abandonment rates.
* Increase investment in high-performing acquisition channels.
* Implement remarketing campaigns for cart abandoners.
* Improve website engagement to reduce bounce rates.
* Develop targeted retention programs for at-risk customers.
* Use customer feedback to enhance the overall customer experience.

---

## Conclusion

The Customer Journey & Funnel Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer behavior, conversion performance, and business outcomes. By identifying bottlenecks in the customer journey and evaluating channel effectiveness, organizations can make data-driven decisions to improve customer acquisition, increase conversions, enhance retention, and drive sustainable business growth.
